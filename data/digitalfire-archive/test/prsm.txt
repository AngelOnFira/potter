PRSM - Poisson's Ratio Test
ASTM states: "This test method covers the determination of the elastic properties of ceramic
whiteware
materials. Specimens of these materials possess specific mechanical resonance frequencies which are defined by the elastic moduli, density, and geometry of the test specimen. Therefore the elastic properties of a material can be computed if the geometry, density, and mechanical resonance frequencies of a suitable test specimen of that material can be measured."
