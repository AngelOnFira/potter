-Biocides
-Cadmium chloride
-Ceramic fibres

-Formaldehyde

-Natural rubber latex

-Proxel
-Red

-Releasing oils
-Resins
-Sodium tripolyphosphate
-Turpentine
-Vanadium pentoxide
But let us bear in mind that
studio pottery may be quite different from the pottery industry
when it comes to chemicals and processes used.
