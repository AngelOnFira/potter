Insight-live
shows recipes in tall narrow panels that open right-ward. The wider the monitor the more you can compare. In this video I click the plus-sign arrow for three different recipes (after having done a search in the recipe manager). That opens them (as opposed to the normal preview that would show). Recipes have a link to click to turn calculation on, that is remembered every time they are opened after that. Once they are open notice how easy it is to compare the
chemistry
and recipes.

melting temperature
