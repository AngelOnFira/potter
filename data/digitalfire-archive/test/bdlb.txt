BDLB - Bulk Density lbs/cu. ft. (Packed) Test
The measurement is done on the powdered material after
settling
(settling is accelerated in the test procedure). See ASTM C-29
Use the same procedure as the g/cc bulk density test, however multiply answer by 62.42 to get lbs/cubic foot.
