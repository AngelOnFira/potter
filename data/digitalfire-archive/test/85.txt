AVPS - Average Particle Size (Microns) Test
Particle size analysis falls into two categories: Ultimate and physical. Physical particles are those we can measure using ordinary
sieves
.
Ultimate particles
, in the micron range, require the use of specialized devices.
Variables
VAL - Value (V)
The size in microns of the average particle of a powder sample
