Bamboo Glaze
A high temperature reduction glaze made by adding a small amount of iron oxide to a magnesia matte base glaze
Key phrases linking here:
bamboo glaze, bamboo matte
 -
Learn more
Details
Usually describes a bamboo colored
matte glaze
(especially in reduction
stoneware
). These are normally achieved in a
magnesia matte
white by adding a small amount of iron (0.5-1%) and some
tin oxide
opacifier
.
