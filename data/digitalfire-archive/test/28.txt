HEGF - Hegman Fineness Test
LSTM-17 test method.
This is quoted on
talc
and
kaolin
data sheets, for example.
Here is a good description of the method from gardco.com:
In its simplest conception, a Fineness of Grind Gage may be considered essentially a continuous variable screen. It can be readily seen that if a material such as fine
silica
were distributed uniformly in a single particle thickness in the gage path, these particles would protrude above the sides of the path starting at whatever point that their physical size exceeded the depth of the path.
If a straight edge scraper was brought to bear on the sides of the path in conjunction with the deepest part, and then moved slowly down the path to the shallow end, it would contact the protruding particles and on continued movement, screen or push these particles from the path.
